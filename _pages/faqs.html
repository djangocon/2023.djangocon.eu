Tickets

Can I buy a Virtual ticket to the conference?
Sorry, no. This is an in-person event.
However, all talks will be recorded, and will be made available after the event.
(No ticket required)
I have been accepted as a speaker at DjangoCon Europe 2023. Do I need to buy a conference ticket?
Speakers receive free tickets to the full conference, including the social event.
We will send you a voucher code.
Was there an Early Bird price?
No, the price you see now is the same price as when we opened ticket sales.
We did not want anyone who needed to wait to buy their tickets to pay more.
We feel that this is the easiest way to be fair to our attendees and talk submitters.
Can I cancel my ticket?
Yes you can! More information is on our Tickets page.
